Vision

Help bridge the gap between what students learn at colleges and what enterprises really need.
• Many educated still not employable.

• 0-2 years employment tenure decreasing due to expectation mismatch.
• Students struggling to achieve early success in carrer.
• Corporate engagement in Indian education at infancy.

Benefits

● For Students :
• Make students more employable
• Be more informed on real industry expectations and succeed right from the beginning of their career.

● For Institutions :
• Bring corporate experience into classroom curriculum
• Make institutions more successful in creating employable workforce
